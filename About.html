Nice to Meet You

Here's a little about me.

I have tried my hands at all kinds of work over the years. But it wasn't until I found software engineering that I truly felt at home. I have had a passion for technology from the beginning. My first PC was Frankensteined together from several desktops that were being disposed of. Every video game system (Or electronic device for that matter) that I've ever owned has been disassembled just to see the inner workings.

Striving to find the easiest ways to do everything I fell in love with excel and, naturally, that evolved into coding. Whether it’s for budgeting or making apps to help my family keep track of things in video games, I love using my time to find a way to save others’ theirs.
